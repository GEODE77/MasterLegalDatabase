Basis and Purpose Statement: 
The regulations in this chapter are being moved into Parks and Wildlife Chapter W-16 - “Parks and 
Wildlife Procedural Rules” 2 CCR 406-16. These changes are a cleanup to consolidate the division agent 
regulations into one chapter instead of being in two different chapters. 
The statements of basis and purpose for these regulations can be obtained from the Colorado Division of 
Parks and Wildlife, Office of the Regulations Manager by emailing dnr_cpw_planning@state.co.us or by 
visiting the Division of Parks and Wildlife headquarters at 6060 Broadway, Denver, CO, 80216.
