History 
Entire rule repealed eff. 07/15/2014. 
Annotations 
The Committee on Anticompetitive Conduct (CAC) regulations limits the scope of the CAC's 
jurisdiction. CAC was not intended to be the repository of all claims of anticompetitive conduct between 
a physician and a hospital. As a result, the CAC's jurisdiction encompasses only antitrust allegations 
arising out of adverse decisions regarding physician competency. Ryals v. St. Mary-Corwin Regional 
Med. Ctr., 10 P.3d 654 (2000).
