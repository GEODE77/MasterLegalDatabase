Editor’s Notes 
In 2003, the State Board of Education approved the Rules for the Administration of Colorado Online 
Programs (1 CCR 301-56). With the passage of SB07-215, which repealed former statute regarding 
online program requirements, and with the promulgation of the 2008 Rules for the Administration, 
Certification and Oversight of Online Programs (1 CCR 301-71), the repeal of 1 CCR 301-56 is necessary 
in order to comport with current statute. 
History 
Entire rule repealed eff. 11/30/2008.
