Editor’s Notes 
History 
Entire rule eff. 03/30/2011. 
Entire rule repealed eff. 10/01/2011. 
Annotations 
The authority for 1 CCR 101-2 is CRS 24-30-203.5(4)(a), which the Legislature revised in HB 11-
1307 to delete rule making for recovery audits.  Since the Legislature deleted rulemaking for recovery 
audits, the State Controller is revoking the rules that were issued pursuant to CRS 24-30-203.5(4)(a).
