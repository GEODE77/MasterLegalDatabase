Statement of Basis and Purpose 
Adopted September 20, 2017 - Effective November 30, 2017 
Statutory Authority 
The Commissioner's authority for the adoption of this permanent Rule amendment is set forth in § 35-60-
109(1) C.R.S. 
Purpose 
The Purpose of this rulemaking is to repeal the Rule published at 8 CCR 1202-3 in their entirety. 
Factual and Policy Issues 
In 2000 article 60 of Title 35 was repealed and re-enacted causing a change that made this rule obsolete. 
Current statutory provisions as well as 8 CCR 1202-6 “Rules for Commercial Feed Under the Colorado 
Feed Law, Sections 35-60-101 through 115, C.R.S.” have made regulations in this rule duplicative and no 
longer necessary. Repealing this rule would clean up the Commercial Feed regulations that are currently 
in effect.  This repeal is a result of the Departments Regulatory Review Process.
