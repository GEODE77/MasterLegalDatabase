2243.7-R-1.00  Statement of Basis and Purpose 
The statute authorizing the School District Capital Construction Assistance Program, established in 
sections 22-43.7-101 through 106, C.R.S., was repealed and reenacted in 2008, and these rules are 
therefore no longer applicable.
