2 CCR 405-6

_________________________________________________________________________ 
Basis and Purpose: 
This chapter is being eliminated with previous P-6 regulations being moved into other Parks and Wildlife 
chapters. Specifically, “#600 Declaratory Orders” is being combined with the declaratory orders section 
within Chapter W-16, creating one “Procedural Rules” chapter for both sides of the agency (Parks and 
Wildlife). All of the hunting special use permit regulations for Lone Mesa (#601) and Golden Gate (#602) 
State Parks have been moved into Chapter P-7 since they are a type of customer permit and logically 
should be located in that chapter.
