DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION 
COLORADO STATE BOARD OF EDUCATION 
RULES FOR THE ADMINISTRATION OF THE ACCESSIBLE EDUCATIONAL MATERIALS FOR 
STUDENTS WITH PRINT DISABILITIES GRANT PROGRAM - Repealed eff. 12/30/2020 
1 CCR 301-70 
[Editor’s Notes follow the text of the rules at the end of this CCR Document.] 
_________________________________________________________________________ 
C.R.S. 22-88-102, which authorized the Accessible Educational Materials for Students with Print 
Disabilities Grant Program, was repealed by the Colorado General Assembly in 2010. The corresponding 
rules in 1 CCR 301-70 have become obsolete and are being repealed by the Colorado State Board of 
Education.
