17.100  PARENTAL FEE FOR JUVENILE PLACEMENTS [Rev. eff. 7/1/12] 
When a juvenile is committed to the Department, the Department may, based upon the parent’s ability to 
pay, require the parent of the child to pay a fee to cover the costs of the placement. The Department shall 
utilize the parental fee requirements found at Section 7.404, et seq. (12 CCR 2509-5), as applicable to 
each juvenile in placement.
